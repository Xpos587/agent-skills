# Related Rights (Смежные права)

Art. 1303–1334 Civil Code. Protect rights adjacent to copyright — for performers, phonogram producers, broadcasters, and database makers.

## Protected Objects

| Object | Rightsholder | Term |
|---|---|---|
| Performance | Performer | 50 years from performance |
| Phonogram | Phonogram producer | 50 years from publication/fixation |
| Broadcasting | Broadcasting organization | 50 years from broadcast |
| Database (sui generis) | Database maker | 15 years from creation |
| Publication of previously unpublished work | First lawful publisher | 25 years from publication |

**Verify terms**: ConsultantPlus → Art. 1303–1334 ГК РФ.

## Key Differences from Copyright

| Aspect | Copyright | Related Rights |
|---|---|---|
| Object | Original creative work | Performance, recording, broadcast |
| Creativity required | Yes (presumed) | Lower threshold (effort, investment) |
| Moral rights | Strong | Limited (performer: name, integrity) |
| Term | Life + 70 years | 50 years (fixed from event) |

## Performers' Rights

- Moral: right to be identified, protection from distortion
- Exclusive: record, broadcast, distribute, make available online

## Phonogram Producers' Rights

- Right to use phonogram (broadcast, public performance, distribution, online)
- **Remuneration right**: broadcast/public performance → producer AND performers get remuneration via VOIS

## Broadcasting Organizations' Rights

Simultaneous rebroadcast, communicate to public, fix, reproduce fixation.

## Database Sui Generis Right

Distinct from copyright in database structure. Protects **investment** in obtaining/verifying/presenting contents.

- Applies when substantial financial/material/organizational investment
- Prevents extraction/reuse of substantial parts
- **Key case: VKontakte** — 5-year litigation: social network database rights when users input data? Court recognized VK's investment in structuring/maintaining.

## Free Uses

Quotation, news reporting, personal use, educational. Verify current list: Art. 1306 ГК РФ.

## Common Mistakes

- Performers ≠ recording owners (typically producer/label holds phonogram rights)
- Forgetting collective remuneration via VOIS
- Confusing database copyright with sui generis right → different criteria/terms
- Related rights require no registration
